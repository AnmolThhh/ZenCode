ZenCore: The Guilt-Free Productivity Workspace

We all get distracted—switching tabs to check a message or watch a quick video when we should be working. Traditional timers punish you by resetting your streak or flashing a failure screen, which just kills your motivation. ZenCore is a productivity command center built to fix that by embracing imperfect focus.

It works silently in the background. If you leave your workspace to browse another tab, ZenCore detects it, logs the duration as "Stray Time," and welcomes you back without the guilt trip when you return.

How to Use It
Because ZenCore relies on the browser's native visibility sensors, it adapts perfectly to different workflows without needing a complex settings menu:

    The Developer Workflow (Split-Screen): Snap your local code editor to one side of your screen and ZenCore to the other or keep the app running in the background and the editor in minimized mode. Because the browser window remains visible on your monitor, it tracks your entire coding session as uninterrupted Focus Time.

    The Deep Work Workflow (Full-Screen): For studying, writing, or reading documentation, keep ZenCore maximized in your browser. If you get distracted and open a new tab for social media or YouTube, ZenCore silently notices, logs the "Stray Time," and shows you your honest stats when you return.

Under the Hood
The system is a 100% client-side web application.

    The Tech Stack: Built purely with React, Vite, and Tailwind CSS for a lightning-fast, minimalist UI.

    AI-Architected: Engineered using agent-driven development inside Google Antigravity (powered by Claude Sonnet 4.6).

    Zero Server Dependency: It relies entirely on the HTML5 Page Visibility API to track your attention and securely saves all your session analytics in your browser's local storage. There are no databases, no backends, and absolutely zero runtime costs.
